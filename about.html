Who Am I?

Hi, I’m Pradipti Chakraborty, a final-year BCA student at Adamas University, passionate about designing and web development. With a deep interest in technology, I am constantly honing my skills in front-end and back-end development. This blog is my way of sharing my journey and growth in the field of web development and digital design.

My Mission

Through this blog, my aim is to offer helpful insights and tips to fellow students and aspiring web developers. Whether you're just starting or looking to refine your skills, you'll find practical advice here on everything from coding to design and career development in the tech world.

What You'll Find Here

This blog covers a range of topics I’m passionate about:

  • Web Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more.
  • Design: Creative techniques and tips to design aesthetically pleasing websites.
  • Tech Insights: The latest trends in the tech world, including tools, frameworks, and innovations.
  • Personal Growth: Tips on balancing studies, coding, and pursuing personal passions.

Hobbies and Interests

When I’m not coding or learning about design, I enjoy exploring new places, diving into books, and indulging in good food. Technology, innovation, and digital art inspire me, and I’m always looking to expand my horizons in these fields.
